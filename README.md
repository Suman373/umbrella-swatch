### Customize Your Umbrella

- **Choose Colors:** Use the **color swatches** to change the color of the umbrella fabric and the application's theme.

- **Upload Logo:** Click the **upload icon** to instantly preview your logo on the umbrella's surface.

- **Remove Design:** Click the **'X' delete button** to easily clear the logo from the preview.
